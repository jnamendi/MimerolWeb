T-Connect

Receive orders directly on your computer? If you have a computer with an internet connection in your restaurant, you don't have to worry anymore about your email or fax machine. Orders will be sent automatically to our T-Connect software on your computer. If you prefer, the orders can be automatically printed as well.

Click on the link below to download T-Connect, preferably on the machine on which you'll be using the T-Connect software. After the installation our callcenter team will create a connection to your restaurant.
